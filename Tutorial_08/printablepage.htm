Chocolate Cake Recipe

This classic chocolate cake is rich, moist, and perfect for celebrations!

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine dry ingredients.
  3. Add eggs, buttermilk, oil, and vanilla. Mix well.
  4. Stir in hot water until batter is smooth (it will be thin).
  5. Pour into pans and bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool for 10 minutes in the pan, then remove to cool completely on a wire rack.
